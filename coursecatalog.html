COURSE CATALOG

A project for redesigning UT course catalog website

OVERVIEW

This is an ongoing project. The aim of the project is to enhance the students' experiences for course searching and class registration. I will keep updating in this semster. You are welcomed to come back to check the progress.

DESIGN PROCESS

BACKGORUND

Although its importance for every UT student, unfortuantely the UT course catalog has long been complained for its outdated and lack of integration. The course search criteria used by the current website do not meet the needs for more precise filtering. In addition, there is no further action student can take on the search result page, such as adding to planner or bookmarking. Students still have to take notes of the course numbers by hand and put those numbers on the registration web page, which is seperated from the course search website.

As the students of UT, we know those painpoints well. It intrigures us to initiate a redesign project of the UT course catalog. On the other hand, from the perspective of Information Architecture, we are also interested in what and how students usually look for when composing their class schedule. We are going to take a both content and user centered approach to tackle those research questions.
