ME UNI.

A meditation app helping people keep meditating

OVERVIEW

Me Uni. is a mobile app aiming to help people build a habit of meditation. It features a fun and engaging game to motivate users to keep the practice, a robust social platform allowing users to connect to the meditation community, and a heart beat tracker to watch their body change. Through Me Uni., users can form a lasting habit of meditation, enjoy doing it alone or join the thriving community of meditators around the globe.

    BACKGORUND

    Meditation has long been under scientific investigation and proven beneficial to mental health. It is not just effective; even few minutes of meditation can improve resilience, it is also easy to learn. However, for people considering attending a meditation class, time and location are always two big challenges.

    Many meditation apps have brought their solutions to address those challenges. While after surveying the main players in the market, we found there are still some shortcomings within the current solutions. Therefore, we decided to propose our solution based on our research.

    COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

    We selected three direct competitors with most users and three indirect competitors as the targets of our competitive analysis. We listed the features they contain and compared the way they organize information. We also reviewed usersâ€™ comments and ratings. After those close examination, we were able to generalized some takeaways about what are provided and what are missed in the existing solutions.

    TAKEAWAYS

    Most competitors provide a varieties of content, such as audio, music, broadcast, even video. Some classify their content by different purposes (e.g. anxiety, relationship). Some classify their content according to different groups of audience (e.g. begineer, kid).

    Most competitors provide self tracking information such as the stats of continued time. To motivate users to keep practice, some of them use ranking board, while some use badges. All competitors allow setting personal reminder (and usually will recommend to do so).

    Very few competitors include social community feature. Most of them have very limited channel to reach out other users or mentors. In this catalog, Insight Timer makes it standing out from the others. It's more like meditator's version of Facebook. The social community on Insight Timer is strong and users become closer through experience sharing and interpersonal communication.
