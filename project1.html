Me Uni.

A meditation app helping people keep meditating

Overview

Me Uni. is a mobile app aiming to help people build a habit of meditation. It features a fun and engaging game to motivate users to keep the practice, a robust social platform allowing users to connect to the meditation community, and a heart beat tracker to watch their body change. Through Me Uni., users can form a lasting habit of meditation, enjoy doing it alone or join the thriving community of meditators around the globe.
